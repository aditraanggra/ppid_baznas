# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

**Primary:** Masyarakat umum (citizens, journalists, auditors) who need to access public information about BAZNAS Cianjur's ZIS fund management, submit information requests, and track their status. They arrive with a specific information need and expect clarity, speed, and official credibility.

**Secondary:** Admin PPID staff who process permohonan (information requests) under strict SLA deadlines. They need efficient workflows, clear status visibility, and zero ambiguity in their daily operations.

Both user groups share a need for trust: citizens need to trust that BAZNAS is transparent and accountable; admins need the system to reflect that institutional standard in every interaction.

## Product Purpose

Fulfill BAZNAS Kabupaten Cianjur's legal obligation under UU No. 14/2008 (Keterbukaan Informasi Publik) by providing a digital platform for public information disclosure and information request management. Success means: citizens can find, request, and track public information without friction; admins can process requests within SLA without confusion; and the platform itself communicates institutional transparency and credibility through its design quality.

## Brand Personality

**Trustworthy** — Official, credible, reliable. This is a government transparency platform; every design choice must reinforce institutional trust.

**Professional** — Competent, serious, efficient. Clean information architecture, clear hierarchy, no wasted space.

**Modern** — Contemporary, clean, well-crafted. Not a dated government template. Digital-first quality that matches 2026 standards.

**Friendly** — Approachable, warm, helpful. Not cold or bureaucratic. Citizens should feel welcomed, not intimidated by government process.

## Anti-references

- **Generic government templates** — Cluttered, bureaucratic, text-heavy government sites with poor navigation and dated aesthetics.
- **SaaS dashboard aesthetic** — Cold, tech-startup look (gradient cards, dark mode by default, neon accents) that doesn't feel official or institutional.
- **Overly decorative / flashy** — Excessive animations, parallax, or design flourishes that undermine trust and seriousness.
- **Cream/parchment/paper backgrounds** — The saturated AI warm-neutral trend. This is a modern government platform, not a vintage document.

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity over cleverness** — Every citizen should understand what to do within 3 seconds of landing on any page. No clever metaphors, no hidden navigation, no ambiguous labels.

2. **Institutional craft** — The quality of the interface IS the message. A well-crafted, pixel-precise platform communicates that BAZNAS takes its transparency obligation seriously. Sloppy design = sloppy governance in the public's mind.

3. **Accessible by default** — WCAG 2.1 AA is the floor, not the ceiling. High contrast, clear focus states, logical tab order, meaningful alt text. This serves everyone, not just users with disabilities.

4. **Progressive disclosure** — Show what's needed now, reveal complexity on demand. Multi-step forms, expandable sections, clear information hierarchy. Don't overwhelm citizens with process details they don't need yet.

5. **Respect the user's time** — Fast loads, minimal clicks to goal, clear status feedback at every step. A citizen tracking their permohonan should see status in one screen, not three.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance as baseline
- High contrast ratios on all text (≥4.5:1 body, ≥3:1 large text)
- Keyboard navigation support throughout
- Screen reader compatible with meaningful ARIA labels
- Reduced motion support for all animations
- Color-blind safe information encoding (never color-only for status)
- Bahasa Indonesia as primary language; clear, simple vocabulary for public-facing text
